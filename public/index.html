About Retchi

Retchi is a powerful Point of Sale (POS) solution designed to streamline the operations of small pet shops. With Retchi, you can manage stock reports, sales reports, and transactions all in one place, ensuring that you always have real-time insights into your business. Accessible anytime and anywhere, Retchi helps pet shop owners stay on top of their inventory and sales performance with ease. The app is compatible with Android devices, making it the perfect mobile solution for small business owners who want efficiency, simplicity, and control at their fingertips.
